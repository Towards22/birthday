BACKGROUND MUSIC SETUP
======================

To enable the background birthday music feature:

1. Obtain a birthday song MP3 file (ensure you have the rights to use it)
2. Name it exactly: birthday-song.mp3
3. Place it in this /public folder
4. The music player in the Hero section will automatically detect and use it

Recommended song length: 2-4 minutes
Recommended file size: Under 5MB for optimal loading

Popular choices:
- Traditional "Happy Birthday" instrumental
- Upbeat celebratory music
- Meaningful song to the celebrant

Note: The audio will only play after user interaction (clicking the "Play Birthday Music" button)
to comply with browser autoplay policies.
